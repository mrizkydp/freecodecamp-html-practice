Recipe: Nasi Goreng Kampung

Nasi Goreng Kampung is a fast food dish that uses cold rice (preferably left over from the night before) to prevent it from becoming mushy, sautéed with shallots/garlic, eggs, sweet soy sauce, and accompaniments such as shredded chicken or meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the shallots and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Push the vegetables to the side of the wok and crack the eggs into the center. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  4. Add the cooked rice and sweet soy sauce, and stir to combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
